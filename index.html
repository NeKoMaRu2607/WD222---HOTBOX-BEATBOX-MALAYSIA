About Us

HOTBOXMALAYSIA™ is an organization that focuses on providing a stable and wholesome environment for all beatbox artists throughout Malaysia. We lead the community by guiding and educating them with the best modern pieces of beatbox information. As a result, they can improve their skills and represent Malaysia as professional beatbox artists. Aside from that, we organized some of the largest beatbox events in Malaysia like the HOTBOX Beatbox Battle which is for the community to showcase their skills, creativity, and unique style. We film our beatbox content with our equipment and share it with the world to inspire everyone who watches it. Our creative content can be seen on our YouTube channel and across all social networks; Instagram, TikTok, and others.

Our Goals

Our ultimate goal is to serve as Malaysia's Central Beatbox Platform that instills community values and protects beatbox artists as they rise to fame, inspiring everyone who watches our performances, battles, and content to beatbox themselves. Besides that, we persist in motivating featured artists with reasons to keep pushing the art form of beatboxing and thus showing them, that they are the avant-garde of vocal music. Redesigning what we know about the limitations of the human vocals. We are not just documenting, we are leading this revolutionary vocal music culture, through the art form of beatbox.
